Making A Sandwich

  1. Gather preferred bread & filling(s), plate, & required utensils onto work surface
  2. Remove two slices of bread from packaging and place onto the plate
  3. Open filling(s) and use utensils if necessary, to apply the filling(s) onto the bread
  4. Place one piece of bread on top of the other
  5. Close fillings and bread package
  6. Return bread and filling(s) to their proper place
  7. Rinse utenstils and place in sink or dishwasher for later washing
